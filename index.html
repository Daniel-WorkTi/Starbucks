It's Not Just Coffee
It's Starbucks

The Frappuccino is an Iconic Creation from Starbucks, known for its blend of iced Coffee, milk, ice, and syrup, all mixed to create a creamy and refreshing beverage. The word "Frappuccino" is a combination of "frozen" and "cappuccino," indicating the frozen nature and coffee base of the drink.
